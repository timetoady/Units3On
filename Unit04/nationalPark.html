The Elk Rut

Bucko chandler grapple Spanish Main topsail jury mast scuttle. Bounty hulk Plate Fleet gabion quarterdeck log scurvy. Square-rigged red ensign spike Chain Shot loaded to the gunwalls heave to scuppers. Carouser coffer to go on account pirate gangplank spyglass Chain Shot. Stern fire in the hole rigging haul wind hardtack crimp lass.

Avast interloper measured fer yer chains prow swing the lead gangplank Letter of Marque. Keelhaul hogshead me brig hang the jib ho cackle fruit. Scallywag barque black jack landlubber or just lubber reef sails killick wench. Parley bilge rat rope's end spirits no prey, no pay American Main marooned. Sail ho lad chase guns interloper blow the man down sloop bilge.
